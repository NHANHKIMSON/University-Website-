Academic Services

Student Support Services

  • Advising & Counseling: Personal and academic advising to guide you through your academic journey.
  • Career Services: Assistance with internships, job placement, resume building, and career counseling.
  • Tutoring & Mentorship: Access to tutors and peer mentors in various subjects.

  • Library: A fully equipped library with digital and physical resources.
  • Sports & Recreation: Access to gyms, fitness centers, and sports teams.
  • Student Housing: On-campus dormitories and housing options for students.

Community Engagement

  • Continuing Education: Short courses, workshops, and certificates for professional and personal development.
  • Research & Innovation: Cutting-edge research opportunities in various fields of study.
  • Public Events & Lectures: Regular seminars, guest lectures, and workshops.
